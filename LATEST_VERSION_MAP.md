# Sovereign Ledger - Latest Version Map

This document outlines the final selection of prototype screens for the AI First Banker application, as well as the archived older iterations. The selection was made based on the most refined workflow-oriented screens, maximizing consistency through the `aligned_nav` versions.

### Dashboard

**Selected:** `01_dashboard` (formerly `rm_copilot_workbench_aligned_nav`)
**Archived:** 
- `1._rm_home_dashboard`
- `relationship_manager_portal`
- `rm_ai_copilot_workbench`
- `rm_copilot_operational_workbench_v2`

**Reason:** This version provides the most unified and refined Copilot workspace. It utilizes the standardized `aligned_nav` architecture, ensuring consistent side-navigation across the suite. It features the most comprehensive AI Morning Brief and operational metrics layout.

### Customer 360

**Selected:** `02_customer_360` (formerly `techcorp_solutions_workbench_aligned_nav`)
**Archived:** 
- `2._customer_360_view`
- `relationship_workbench_techcorp_solutions`
- `relationship_workbench_techcorp_solutions_v2`
- `relationship_workbench_techcorp_solutions_v3`

**Reason:** This version aligns with the `aligned_nav` structure and presents a highly operational "relationship workbench." It is significantly more polished than the earlier portfolio-style or iterative workbench versions, providing better operational clarity on blockers and tasks.

### AI Meeting Brief

**Selected:** `03_ai_meeting_brief` (formerly `ai_meeting_brief_aligned_nav`)
**Archived:** 
- `3._ai_meeting_brief`

**Reason:** Successfully migrates the core AI Meeting Brief functionality into the standardized navigation shell, ensuring it fits perfectly into the final prototype flow.

### Post-Meeting Follow-up

**Selected:** `04_post_meeting_follow_up` (formerly `post_meeting_follow_up_aligned_nav`)
**Archived:** 
- `4._post_meeting_follow_up`

**Reason:** Adopted for consistency. It uses the `aligned_nav` framework to maintain a cohesive user experience as part of the post-meeting automation story.

### Documents Workbench

**Archived:** 
- `documents_workbench`

**Reason:** The `aligned_nav` version provides a more integrated experience with the overall dashboard design and features a cleaner, more readable data-dense table for tracking missing documents.

### Supporting / Reference Assets
